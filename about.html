About

The band Upon Wonder was founded in early 2016 with the members Emma, ​​Sarah, Jenna and Laura and broke through with their first album "Wonder" containing the top songs "Great Things" and "Don't Mind Me". Since then they've released three more albums and have toured the world and played in some of the biggest arenas.
The bands music has over 5 billion streams and is considered to be Sweden's largest music export of all time. In 2018 guitarist Maya came along with the group and the girls have been increasing in popularity even more since then.

Emma Instagram

Emma was born on June 14th in 1992 in Stockholm, Sweden, where she also grew up. Emma was discovered on the swedish television show Idol in 2015 where she performed the song "You Oughta Know" by Alanis Morissette.

After the show, where she ended up in 7th place, she got in touch with her manager Roger Edwards and together they came up with the idea of starting the girl band.

Besides her passions for singing and playing the guitar, Emma also has an interest in step dance and has won several national competitions in her early teens.

Sarah Instagram

Sarah was born on July 10th in 1993 in a small town just outside of Stockholm. Her singing career started at the age of 13 when she starred in a musical directed by her father George Andersson.

At the age of 14 she was signed to her first record label where she released her first album. After three packed summers of touring the country she decided to get back and focus on school and It wasn't until early 2016 she decided to take up her passion for singing again and join the band.

Jenna Instagram

Jenna was born on May 8th in 1989 in Copenhagen, Denmark, and moved to Stockholm with her mother and four brothers when she was eight years old. Her mother taught her to play the piano at a very early age and Jenna decided to attend the Royal College of Music in Stockholm to continue with her passion for the instrument.

Her singing has always come natural for Jenna so when she heard there was a girl band looking for a singer/piano player she didn't hesitate to aply for the job.

Laura Instagram

Laura was born on December 17th in 1991 in Sundsvall, Sweden. She inherited her interest for music from her father John Wallin, who was a music teacher at the local school. She managed to master both guitar and drums and used to perform with her dad at local events in town.

Laura attended high school in Stockholm where she studied arts and craft but was always active in several music oriented subjects on the nearby cultural schools.

Laura met Emma during her participation in Idol in 2015, but by that time Laura wasn't interested in joining a band. After a bit of persuasion from both Emma and Roger she decided to finally try on the life as a member of a girl band.

Maya Instagram

Maya is the newest member of the girl group. She came along with the girls in May 2019 when the band was looking for a new bass player. Maya was born on January 2nd in 1992 in Stockholm but grew up together with her grandparents on their farm in Nynäshamn.

Mayas grandfather teached her to play both the guitar and bass, but singing has always been her great passion as well. Maya also attended the swedish television show Idol in 2018 where she ended up in 4th place after a nerve wracking duel between her and Johanna Wikingsson after they both ended up with the same voting score.

Even if she didn't win the duel over Johanna, Maya has won the hearts of all Wonder fans since she became one in the Wonder team.
